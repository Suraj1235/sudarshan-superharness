# Code Of Conduct

Sudarshan is an engineering project where rigorous disagreement is welcome and
personal hostility is not.

Contributors and maintainers are expected to:

- discuss ideas, code, and evidence rather than attacking people
- make space for different experience levels and communication styles
- give actionable feedback and accept correction in good faith
- avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct, or publishing
  another person's private information

Project maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions and participation that
violate these expectations. Serious or sensitive conduct reports should be sent
privately through the repository's security advisory channel. Maintainers will review
reports as promptly and impartially as practical and may warn, temporarily restrict,
or permanently exclude participants when needed to protect the community.
